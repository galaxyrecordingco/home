// FAQ

Common Questions

Do you work with clients outside of Pennsylvania? +
Absolutely — in fact, most of our mixing and mastering work is done remotely. You upload your files through a secure link, we get to work, and deliver your finished audio digitally. No geographic limits whatsoever.
What file formats do you accept? +
We accept WAV, AIFF, and MP3. For stems, we strongly prefer 24-bit WAV files at 44.1 kHz or higher. We'll send you a clear checklist of exactly what to prepare after you book.
How long does a mix or master take? +
Standard turnaround is 48–72 hours for single tracks. Rush delivery (24 hr) is available at a small premium. EP and album projects are quoted individually with a delivery timeline agreed upfront — we never leave you guessing.
How do revisions work? +
Each plan includes a set number of revisions. Submit your feedback clearly — in writing or voice memo — and we'll revise within 24 hours. We want you to be proud of the final product and won't stop until it's right.
Can you handle live events and AV for businesses? +
Yes — AV tech and live sound engineering is a core part of what we do. We've worked corporate events, music venues, conferences, and houses of worship. Contact us for a custom quote on any live or installation project.
Do you offer ongoing / retainer pricing? +
Yes. If you're a podcast producer, label, or business with regular audio needs, we offer discounted monthly retainer pricing. Reach out and we'll put together a package that fits your volume and budget.
