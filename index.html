// FAQ

Common Questions

Do you work with clients outside of Pennsylvania?
Absolutely — in fact, most of our mixing and mastering work is done remotely. You upload your files through a secure link, we get to work, and deliver your finished audio digitally. No geographic limits.
What file formats do you accept?
We accept WAV, AIFF, MP3, and most common DAW session formats. For stems, we prefer 24-bit WAV files at 44.1kHz or higher. We'll tell you exactly what to send after booking.
How long does a mix or master take?
Standard turnaround is 48-72 hours for single tracks. Rush delivery (24 hr) is available at a small premium. EP and album projects are quoted individually with a delivery timeline agreed upfront.
How do revisions work?
Each plan includes a set number of revisions. Submit your feedback clearly and we'll revise within 24 hours. We want you to be happy with the final product — we work until it's right.
Can you handle live events and AV for businesses?
Yes — AV tech and live sound engineering is one of our core service areas. We've worked corporate events, music venues, conferences, and houses of worship. Contact us for a custom quote on any live or installation project.
